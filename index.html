Services

The Foundation was created in Port-au-Prince, Haiti, Delmas, August 15, 2016, by a group of young Haitian who witnessed the vatious problems within the country. Who believed that they can used their education and their love for the country to make a difference in the life of the people, especially the most vulnerable. This group of young people desired to help people who could not help themselves, specially in education ,food insecurity and create programs that will help develop their neighborhood.
